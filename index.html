Our newly constructed units are built to the highest standards, including triple-pane windows and additional wall insulation. Choose from spacious 2-bedroom, 3-bathroom units with expansive balconies or larger 3 or 4-bedroom, 3-bathroom units spread across three floors. Each unit covers approximately 2,000 sq. ft., not including the private two-car garage. For your convenience, there is a full bathroom on each floor. The modern, upscale kitchens feature Bosch and Miele appliances.

All our units come furnished with furniture. Some units also come fully ready for occupancy with all bedding, kitchenware, etc for immediate move-in. Rental prices include internet, water, sewage, and garbage. Tenancies longer than one month are responsible for their own electricity and gas. Pricing varies based on duration and occupancy.
